Frequently Asked Questions.

Product



What is a chatbot?


A chatbot is an application hosted on a messaging platform which you can communicate with through text or voice messages. Think of it as an app that you can chat to.





Why is Enni a chatbot?


We feel chatbots are the future. Enni being a chatbot allows us to make her smarter. Enni really is your personal assistant.




Where is Enni hosted?


Initally Enni will be hosted on Facebook Messenger. But keep an eye out for Enni popping up on other platforms in the future.




When can I use Enni?


Enni is currently under development. We will be releasing a BETA product soon. Like our Facebook page (you can click the icon below) and we'll be in touch when we're ready to release Enni for BETA testing.





Business



How much does it cost to use Enni?


Enni will be absolutely free for you to use.




Where does the name come from?


Enni in Hungarian means 'to eat'. Hungry - Hungarian ... get it?




Where are we based?


Our team is made up of students or recent graduates from the University of Edinburgh and we continue to be based in Edinburgh. The business was born during a trip to California so we sometimes claim to be a silicon valley startup.




Data



What are your data polices?


We commit to being open and transparent with our use of data. As we will not charge for our product, we may sell data to generate revenue. However any data we sell will be at the aggregate level and will be anonymous. We will not sell your individual data, but instead we will provide sector insights and analysis with the help of your data to help cut food waste further.
